Project Examples

Undead Postal Service

From GameJolt Page:"Undead Postal Service is a pixel art metroidvania set in the zombie apocalypse where society relies on mail carriers to deliver messages for communication." My role on Undead Postal Service was as a level and gameplay designer. I planned and constucted the layout of a significant portion of the playable space, which features unlockable shortcuts and an interconnected design reminiscent of games like Hollow Knight or Dark Souls. I was also involved in the ideation of several core mechanics such as the player's dog companion.

Spell Command

Spell Command is a fantasy first-person action game where you use different combinations of spells to defeat your enemies. I worked on this game as a level designer and systems programmer.
